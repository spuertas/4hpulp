Pulp: Please Use Less Plastic

What's the problem?

Plastic bags and other items are being used once and wasted, contributing to pollution of the environment.

Simple ways to make an impact:

- use reusable grocery bags

- use a refillable water bottle (and tupperware, non-paper plates, etc.)

- reuse or repurpose your plastic bags and other plastic items

- pledge to use less plastic below!
